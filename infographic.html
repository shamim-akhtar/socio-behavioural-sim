Leader Identification Logic (Sec 3.4)

Leaders are chosen based on the composition of the society:

  • Case A If Rank 1 individuals < 50%:
    All Rank 1 individuals become leaders.
  • Case B If Rank 1 individuals > 50%:
    Only Rank 1 individuals with better than average objective values become leaders.
